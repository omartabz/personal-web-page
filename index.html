City:Tokyo,Japan

Japan has a long and fascinating history, with traditions that are still practiced today. You can visit ancient temples and shrines, stroll through serene gardens, or attend a traditional tea ceremony. Japan is home to stunning natural landscapes, from snow-capped mountains and serene bamboo forests to pristine beaches and vibrant coral reefs. You can hike in the Japanese Alps, go skiing in Hokkaido, or relax on a beach in Okinawa. Lastly the variety of food ranges from sushi, sashimi, tempura, ramen, and many other dishes.

Foreign Language: Japanese

The most widely spoken language in Japan is Japanese , called Nihongo (日本語) by the indigenous people. here are actually several dialects of Japanese spoken throughout the country, but the dialect of Tokyo is considered the standard form. In addition to Japanese, there are a couple of other minority languages spoken in Japan such as Ryukyuan and Ainu.
